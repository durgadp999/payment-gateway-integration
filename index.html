Greater Love Children Home

Greater Love Children Home mainly focused on Holistic Child Development model, we have taken children from various areas in and around the district who really need basic needs to live. Past 8 years we are taken almost 35 children and provide education, shelter, food, Health facilities, Vocational training, Relationship management and Behavior modification for them. We can assure each child come out of from our home, He will be a responsible and dedicated individual in this society. So that many social activists and Professional Social Workers all over Tamilnadu refer children to our home.

Why choose us? - Our Mission

We connect people around the world in the fight to end poverty. Working together, we invest in the lives of children and youth, build the healthy environments they need to thrive, and empower them to create lasting change in their own lives and communities.
